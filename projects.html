Java Game Project 2021

This is a small game the objective is to protect Shreks Swamp from enemies. Kill all the enemies to protect the swamp and reach the final level. In the final level find out how high Shrek can reach by playing Shrek Doodle Jump (Shreklejump). Pressing S will get you to fall faster. Tip when reaching the Shreklejump level do not fall of the platforms, falling off the platforms will cause Shrek to die :( .

Check out the code on Github: https://github.com/John-LucaGIT/adbt167/
